In light of the pandemic, most people were introduced to working from home. No commute time, full comfort, and zero co-workers or bosses to deal with. The WFH life took the world by storm and most don't want to leave this new norm. With the world settling down, companies are urging people to return to the office, which has been sparking quite a negative reaction. Workers are now fighting for their right to work remotely, some have already relocated to a different country.

So, Why Is Remote Work So Important?

I myself have been working remote for approximately 5 yrs now and the list of positive effects are endless! I originally come from the most expensive Canadian city to live in - Toronto. I was working tirelessly just to pay for my basic needs. After years of struggling even when I was making an impressive income, I knew there had to be another way. I moved back in with family and began to use my income to travel and enjoy life while I'm still young. Once I took my first long-term trip, there was no going back. I was hooked.

Remote work gives you an opportunity to explore new cultures and expand your mind. It allows for more fun and freedom without a dreaded morning commute or crazy bills. I will never go back to the old way of doing things after experiencing the beauty of what the world has to offer and what I believe, is the way we were meant to live. I have loved watching more and more companies embrace the remote lifestyle and hope that the working world continues to grow this way.

Everyone deserves a chance to live in a way that makes then feel fulfilled without restriction.
